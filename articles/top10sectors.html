Five particular sectors (Renewable Energies, AI & ML, Biotech & Pharma, Transportation, IT & Cybersecurity) appear to be almost certainly promising for the future. Three other sectors can potentially yield exponentially high returns: Space Exploration, Sustainable Agriculture and Ed Tech & Remote Learning.

Nuances

  • › The same phenomenon as the dot-com bubble in the 2000s' is perhaps happening again.
  • › Stock picking will be crucial, as many companies in these sectors may not survive the ongoing revolution.
  • › Certain sectors will remain essential and are not likely to disappear. Even if they are currently flying under the radar, they have the potential to develop in parallel with technological advancements and eventually emerge as dominant sectors.
  • › Don't bury the declining sectors (fossil energies, physical businesses...) too quickly and easily.
  • › We didn't mention the military industry, although it will likely continue to grow, albeit slowly and over the long term. This growth is influenced by escalating global tensions, increasing resource scarcity (which may lead to wars), and the eventual integration of AI into military equipment.
